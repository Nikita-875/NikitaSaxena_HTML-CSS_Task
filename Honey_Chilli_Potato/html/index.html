Honey Chilli Potato

Honey Chilli Potatoes are a super addictive snack – fried potato fingers are tossed in a sesame honey chilli sauce that’s sweet and spicy and will give you sticky fingers that you’ll be licking.

Preperation Time : 1 Hour

Serving Amount : 3 Bowl

Ingredients

  • potato - 500gm
  • spring onion - 100gm
  • garlic - 10-15 cloves
  • onion - 50gm
  • green chilli - 4-5 chillies
  • capsicum - 100gm
  • plain flour - 4 tbsp
  • Rice flour - 4 tbsp
  • corn flour - 1 tbsp
  • sugar - 1 tsp
  • salt - 2 tsp
  • honey - 1 tpsp
  • sasame seed - 4 tbsp
  • soya sauce - 1 tbsp
  • red chilli sauce - 1 tbsp
  • ketchup - 1 tbsp
  • oil - 500 ml

LET'S START COOKING

step 1 : Cut Vegetables

peel and cut potato lengthwise, chop garlic in small pieces, lengthwise onion, lengthwise capsicum , and spring onion finely, slit green chillies in two pieces.

step 2 : Put Potatoes In Cold Water

After cutting potatoes, keep potatoes in cold water for 5 mins so that it will get more crispy after frying.

step 3 : Boil Potatoes

Put half tea spoon salt in water and boil potatoes for minutes.

step 4 : Strain Potatoes

Strain potatoes in a bowl and let it cool down for about 5 mins.

step 5 : Coat Potatoes

sprinkle 1 tbsp plain flour and 1 tbsp rice flour on potatoes, and coat it well.

step 6 : Make Batter

Take 3 tbsp plain flour and 3 tbsp rice flour and add half tsp salt in a bowl, add sufficient water in it to make thick batter.

step 7 : Heat Oil

Take kadhai and put oil in it, and keep it on high flame to heat the oil for frying.

step 8 : Coat Potatoes and Fry.

Put the coated already potatoes in batter and coat it with the batter completely and then deep fry potatoes in low-medium flame untill it become golden.

step 9 : Take Out And Cool Down

Take out fried potatoes and let it cool down on room temprature for about 10 mins.

step 10 : For Extra Crispy

Deep fry potatoes again to make it extra crispy. fry untill it becoma golden brown in low-medium flame.

step 11 : Roast Sasame Seeds

Take a flat pan and put sasame seeds in it, and roast it on low flame untill it become brown.

step 12 : Heat Oil For Sauce

Take out sasame seed in a plate and let it cool down, now take about tbsp oil in the same pan and heat it on medium flame

step 13 : Add Chopped Vegetables

Add chopped Garlic, Green chillies, sasame seeds and let it get golden brown.

step 14 : Add onion and capsicum.

Add chopped onion and chopped capsicum and stir for 3 mins.

step 15 : Make Sauce

Add 1 tsp sugar and 1 tsp salt Mix it and then add 1 tbsp soya sauce, 1 tbsp red chilli sauce, and 1 tbsp ketchup.

step 16 : Add Cornflour

Add 1 cup of Water and then add 1 tbsp cornflour to make sauce thick.

step 17 : Add Black Pepper.

Add half tsp Black Pepper to get strong penetrating aroma and piney flavor.

step 18 : Add spring onion and honey.

Add half cup spring onion and 1 tbsp honey.

step 19 : Add Fried Potatoes

Now add fried potatoes and cook for about 5 minutes

step 20 : Serve and Garnish

Serve in a bowl and garnish with spring onion and sasame seed.
